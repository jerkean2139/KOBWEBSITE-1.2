# Content Studio - Team Guide

## Getting Started

### Access the Content Studio
1. Go to: `yourdomain.com/content-studio`
2. Enter the admin password when prompted
3. Click "Access Content Studio"

### Access the Newsletter Creator
1. From Content Studio, click **Newsletter Creator** button in the header
2. Or go directly to: `yourdomain.com/admin/newsletter`
3. Same admin password as Content Studio

---

## Dashboard Overview

The Content Studio has 5 tabs:

| Tab | Purpose |
|-----|---------|
| **Dashboard** | Quick stats and recent drafts |
| **Generate** | Create new blog posts |
| **Posts** | View and manage all posts |
| **Topics** | Content topic categories |
| **Channels** | YouTube channels for video suggestions |

---

## Generating a New Blog Post

1. Go to the **Generate** tab (or use Quick Generate on Dashboard)
2. Select a **Content Topic** (optional - AI will pick if left blank)
3. Choose an **Image Provider**:
   - **Gemini** - Good for general images
   - **Leonardo** - High quality artistic images
   - **Replicate** - Alternative AI model
4. Click **Generate Blog Post**
5. Watch the progress as AI:
   - Researches trending content
   - Writes the article in Jeremy's voice
   - Generates a featured image
   - Runs SEO optimization
   - Performs AI review with scoring

---

## Viewing & Editing Posts

### Open a Post
1. Go to the **Posts** tab
2. Click the **eye icon** on any post to preview
3. Click the **pencil icon** to open in edit mode

### Quick Edits
1. Click **Edit** button in the modal header
2. Make changes to:
   - Title
   - Meta Description
   - Full Content (markdown format)
3. Click **Save** to keep changes, or **Cancel** to discard

### Copy Content
Each field has a **copy button** (clipboard icon):
- Click once to copy
- Checkmark appears when copied successfully
- Works for: Title, Meta Description, Content, Tags

---

## Remix Feature

Don't like something? Use Remix to regenerate:

### Remix Content
- Click **Remix** next to the content section
- AI rewrites the article with fresh examples
- Same topic, different angle

### Remix Image
- Click **Remix Image** on the featured image
- Select your preferred image provider first
- New image generates in cinematic/documentary style

---

## Publishing

1. Open a draft post
2. Review the content
3. Click **Publish** button
4. Post goes live on the blog

---

## AI Review Scores

After generation, you'll see scores:

| Score | What It Measures |
|-------|------------------|
| **Copy** | Voice authenticity, readability |
| **SEO** | Keywords, structure, meta tags |
| **Image** | Visual relevance and quality |

- **80+** = Good to go
- **60-79** = Review suggestions
- **Below 60** = Needs improvement

---

## Tips for Best Results

1. **Topic Selection**: Specific topics generate more focused content
2. **Review Before Publishing**: Always read through the content
3. **Use Quick Edits**: Make small tweaks without regenerating
4. **Remix Sparingly**: Each remix uses AI credits
5. **Check Scores**: High-priority suggestions are worth addressing

## Newsletter Creator

The Newsletter Creator is a separate tool for generating bi-weekly newsletters.

### Accessing the Newsletter Creator
- Click the **Newsletter Creator** button in the Content Studio header
- Or navigate directly to: `yourdomain.com/admin/newsletter`

### Creating a Newsletter
1. Click **Generate Newsletter**
2. AI will:
   - Research trending topics in your niche
   - Curate relevant content
   - Write engaging newsletter copy
   - Style the email for professional delivery
3. Review the generated content
4. Make any edits needed
5. Send via email or copy for your email platform

### Newsletter Features
- **AI Research**: Finds trending topics and news in your industry
- **Content Curation**: Summarizes relevant articles and insights
- **Email Styling**: Professional formatting ready for sending
- **Real-time Progress**: Watch as each step completes
- **Email Sending**: Direct integration with email service (Resend)
