MANUMATION — WAR STORIES & PROOF VAULT (REVISED)

Purpose
Credibility, humanity, and grounding.

Never generalize these.
Use specifics.

The Crockpot / Lemonade Pitcher

Used when someone glorifies grind.

Scene:
2020.
Kitchen counter.
Plastic lemonade pitcher.
Stainless steel crockpot.
Hand-pouring sanitizer.
Driving to 14 hardware stores.

Lesson:
You can save the business.
You still can’t build one like that.

Duct tape does not scale.

The DTG Printer Disaster

Used when someone wants more offers.

Scene:
Fundraising business working fine.
Went wide.
Bought a DTG printer.
Ink chemistry failed.
Sleepless nights fixing heads.

Lesson:
More options ≠ more leverage.
Complexity taxes everything.

Frank (The AI Sales Rep)

Used when automation feels impersonal.

Scene:
Weeks of follow-up.
Perfect memory.
Always on time.
Frank wasn’t human.

Lesson:
Consistency builds trust.
Humans build relationships.

The Georgia Business

Used when dysfunction feels “normal.”

Scene:
Post-it notes everywhere.
CEO carrying five roles.

Lesson:
Structure doesn’t limit people.
