Step 4: Create Automation Workflows
Go to Automation → Workflows → Create New Workflow

Workflow 1: Assessment Completion
Trigger: Form Submitted → Select Assessment Form
Actions:

Add Tag: Lead Source: Assessment
Wait 2 minutes
Send Email: "Your Assessment Results"
Wait 1 day
Send SMS: "Thanks for taking the assessment..."
Add to Opportunity Pipeline: "New Leads"
Workflow 2: Newsletter Welcome
Trigger: Form Submitted → Select Newsletter Form
Actions:

Add Tag: Lead Source: Newsletter
Send Email: Welcome email with first newsletter
Wait 3 days
Send Email: "Here's what you might have missed..."
Workflow 3: Waterfall Workshop Signup
Trigger: Form Submitted → Select Waterfall Workshop Form
Actions:

Add Tag: Lead Source: Waterfall Workshop
Send Email: Workshop access instructions
Wait 7 days (if no booking)
Send Email: "Ready for your free strategy call?"
Add to Pipeline: "Workshop Participants"
Workflow 4: Booking Confirmations
Trigger: Appointment Booked → Any Calendar
Actions:

Send SMS: Confirmation with meeting link
Wait until 1 day before
Send Email: Reminder with prep questions
Wait until 1 hour before
Send SMS: "See you in 1 hour!"
Workflow 5: No-Show Follow Up
Trigger: Appointment → Status Changed to No-Show
Actions:

Wait 30 minutes
Send SMS: "Sorry I missed you..."
Send Email: Reschedule link
Wait 3 days
Send SMS: Final reschedule attempt
