# CoreTrack Assessment - Writer's Overview
 
## Executive Summary
 
CoreTrack is a sophisticated assessment platform designed to help entrepreneurs identify operational inefficiencies and determine whether they need a business coach or a fractional Chief AI Officer (CAIO) to scale their business. The tool combines psychological assessment techniques with financial impact calculations to create a powerful lead generation and qualification system.
 
## The Problem It Solves
 
Many business owners hit a growth ceiling where they're drowning in day-to-day operations rather than leading strategically. They're typically losing $1,500-$6,000+ monthly to operational chaos they can't even see. CoreTrack quantifies this "leakage" and provides a clear path forward.
 
## How It Works
 
### Assessment Flow
1. **Gut-Punch Introduction** - Black background, bold messaging: "You're not running your business. It's running you."
2. **Leakage Assessment (14 questions)** - Calculates monthly financial losses across 4 categories:
   - Systems & Tools (disconnected software, manual data entry)
   - Processes & Workflows (dependencies, missed tasks)
   - People & Delegation (bottleneck issues, rework)
   - Strategy & Growth (revenue predictability, missed opportunities)
3. **Milestone Checkpoint** - Shows partial results at halfway point to maintain engagement
4. **Growth Assessment (11 questions)** - Calculates potential gains from automation and optimization
5. **Email Capture** - Unlocks full results while capturing lead information
6. **Personalized Results** - Shows total leakage, potential gains, and recommendation (Coach vs CAIO vs Both)
7. **Call-to-Action** - Books a strategy call with Jeremy
 
### Key Features
- **Real-time Calculators** - Live leakage and gains calculators update as users answer
- **Dynamic Narration** - Contextual messages based on severity (e.g., "You're funding a competitor's growth")
- **Smart Recommendations** - Algorithm determines Coach vs CAIO based on leakage patterns
- **Data Persistence** - Railway PostgreSQL backend with GHL integration
- **Role-based Access** - Admin dashboard for viewing all assessments
 
## Technical Architecture
 
### Frontend (React + TypeScript)
- **Framework**: Vite + React 18 with TypeScript
- **Styling**: Tailwind CSS with custom color scheme (Primary: #D50000)
- **Animations**: Framer Motion for smooth transitions
- **Charts**: Recharts for data visualization
- **Icons**: Lucide React
- **Routing**: React Router with protected routes
 
### Backend (Node.js + Express)
- **Database**: Railway PostgreSQL
- **Authentication**: JWT with bcrypt password hashing
- **API**: RESTful endpoints for assessment data
- **Integration**: Go High Level (GHL) for CRM automation
- **Deployment**: Railway with Docker support
