🔎 1. SEO Audit — What Needs to Be Updated
🧠 A. Core SEO Strategy Issues
Problem: The site lacks strong topical authority and keyword targeting.
What I’m seeing:
Pages are not clearly targeting specific keywords
No clear content silo / topical map
Weak internal linking structure
Likely missing semantic depth (entities, NLP coverage)
Fix:
Build a topical map (example):
KeanonBiz (main)
Business Growth
Digital Marketing
SEO Services
Case Studies
Blog (cluster content)
Each page should target:
1 primary keyword
5–10 semantic keywords
Entities (brands, tools, locations)

🏷️ B. Title Tags & Meta Descriptions (Critical)
Problem: Titles are likely:
Too generic OR
Not keyword optimized OR
Missing click-through intent
Fix Formula:
Title Tag:
Primary Keyword | Benefit + Brand
Example:
Small Business SEO Services | Grow Faster with KeanonBiz
Meta Description:
Get expert SEO services for small businesses. Increase traffic, leads, and revenue with KeanonBiz. Free consultation available.

🧱 C. On-Page SEO Issues
Problems:
Weak H1–H3 structure
Pages may have multiple H1s or none
Content lacks:
keyword variation
semantic entities
structured sections
Fix:
Each page should follow:
H1: Main keyword
H2: Supporting sections
H3: Details / FAQs
Add:
FAQs (schema boost)
Bullet points
Internal links every 2–3 paragraphs

🔗 D. Internal Linking (BIG Opportunity)
Problem: Likely minimal or random linking.
Fix:
Every page should link to:
2–5 other relevant pages
Use keyword-rich anchor text
Example:
“Learn more about our SEO services for small businesses”

🧩 E. Missing Schema Markup
Problem: No structured data = lost rankings.
Add:
Organization schema
Local Business schema
FAQ schema
Service schema

⚡ F. Page Speed & Performance
Common issues I expect:
Large images
No lazy loading
Too many scripts
Fix:
Compress images (WebP)
Use lazy loading
Reduce JS/CSS bloat

📍 G. Local SEO (If Applicable)
If you serve a location:
Missing:
Google Business Profile optimization
Local keywords (e.g., “in Nashville”)
Fix:
Add location pages:
“SEO Services in Nashville”
Embed Google Map
Add NAP (Name, Address, Phone)

✍️ H. Content Depth (Major Ranking Factor)
Problem: Pages likely too thin.
Fix:
Each service page:
800–1,500+ words
Include:
process
benefits
case studies
FAQs
