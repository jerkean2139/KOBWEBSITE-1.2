Can Replit work for SEO? Yes, but with caveats:

Platform	Best For	SEO Strength
Vercel	Dynamic sites, SSR/ISR	⭐⭐⭐⭐⭐ (best for SEO-critical sites)
Netlify	Static/Jamstack sites	⭐⭐⭐⭐⭐ (excellent edge caching)
Replit	Rapid development + deploy	⭐⭐⭐ (good with optimizations)
My recommendation: Keep building on Replit for now. We can optimize it significantly with proper caching headers, static asset handling, and performance tuning. If you find cold-start times hurting your Core Web Vitals after launch, migrating to Vercel later is straightforward since it's a Node/React app.
